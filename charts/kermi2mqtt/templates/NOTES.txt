----------------

2. View the logs:
   kubectl logs -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} -l "app.kubernetes.io/name={{ include "kermi2mqtt.name" . }}" -f

3. Check Home Assistant:
   - Look for "Kermi X-Center" device in Settings > Devices & Services > MQTT
   - You should see climate (Floor Heating), water_heater (Hot Water), and sensor entities

TROUBLESHOOTING:
----------------
- If entities don't appear in HA, check MQTT broker connectivity
- For Modbus errors, verify the X-Center is reachable on the network
- Enable DEBUG logging by setting config.logLevel=DEBUG in values
